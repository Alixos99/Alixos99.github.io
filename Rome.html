ROME

The eternal city of Rome, Italy, stands as a testament to centuries of history and civilization. The Colosseum, Roman Forum, and Pantheon are iconic landmarks that showcase the grandeur of ancient Rome. Vatican City, an independent city-state within Rome, is home to St. Peter's Basilica and the Sistine Chapel. Rome's charming cobblestone streets, delicious cuisine, and the lively atmosphere of its piazzas create an unforgettable experience for visitors.
