ISTANBUL

Straddling Europe and Asia, Istanbul is a city with a rich and diverse cultural heritage. The Hagia Sophia and the Blue Mosque showcase the city's Byzantine and Ottoman influences, while the bustling Grand Bazaar and Spice Bazaar provide a glimpse into its vibrant market culture. The Bosphorus Strait, connecting the Sea of Marmara to the Black Sea, adds a scenic element to this captivating city, creating a bridge between continents.
