BARCELONE

Barcelona, the capital of Catalonia in Spain, is a city known for its distinctive architecture, vibrant street life, and artistic flair. The works of renowned architect Antoni Gaudí, including the Sagrada Família and Park Güell, contribute to the city's unique aesthetic. The lively La Rambla promenade, the historic Gothic Quarter, and the beautiful beaches along the Mediterranean Sea make Barcelona a diverse and captivating destination.
