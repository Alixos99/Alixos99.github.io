PARIS

Known as the "City of Light," Paris is a global icon of art, culture, and romance. The French capital is adorned with iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral. Strolling along the Seine River, exploring charming neighborhoods like Montmartre, and indulging in exquisite French cuisine at sidewalk cafes are quintessential Parisian experiences. The city's rich history, art galleries, and fashion scene contribute to its timeless allure.
